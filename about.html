Our Story

THE SUPER CHURCH is a unique family of believers rooted in God's Holy Word and driven by the force of God's Holy Spirit. This family was birthed by the divine mandate received from God by the founding pastor, REVEREND WALTER TIMOTHY, on Thursday 28th March, 2002, as conveyed by the following scripture:

"Send us around the world with the news of Your saving power and Your eternal plan for all mankind." - Psalm 67:2 (TLB)
This assignment received further clarification from the Holy Spirit on Monday 10th June, 2002 through this verse of scripture:
"And He said, It is a light thing that thou shouldest be my servant to raise up the tribes of Jacob, and to restore the preserved of Israel: I will also give thee for a light to the Gentiles, that thou mayest be My salvation unto the end of the earth." - Isaiah 49:6 (KJV)
Thus was THE SUPER CHURCH birthed on God's Word and by the power of the Holy Spirit.

MEET OUR SENIOR PASTORS

Rev. Walter Timothy

REVEREND WALTER TIMOTHY is a dynamic teacher of God's word, whose teaching ministry dissolves doubts and establishes faith in the hearts of his hearers. He has a passion to see souls saved and for the church to come to maturity. Many have received healing through his healing ministry. Ephesians 4:11-13 is the great motivation for his ministry towards the saints. He is the outstanding author of several books, and through his Television Ministry thousands have been brought to the reality of the victorious life that is in Christ Jesus.

Rev. Gloria Timothy

REVEREND GLORIA TIMOTHY brings a refreshing insight into the ministry of God's Word. Her love for God and her consequent efforts resulted in the expansion of the ministry, as she pioneered the first branch of the church ever. She is a committed wife, a loving mother, and an amazing helper in the ministry of the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ
